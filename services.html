FTL — Full Truckload Transportation

Ideal for large loads from 15,000 to 45,000 kilograms

Service Description

FTL transportation provides a dedicated truck exclusively for your cargo. The trailer travels directly from pickup to delivery without intermediate stops, minimizing handling risk and transit time.

Advantages:

  • Direct delivery without reloading
  • Lower risk of damage
  • Fixed price per shipment
  • Faster transit times

Volume:up to 70 m³

Routes:Germany, France, Poland & more

LTL — Less-than-Truckload Transportation

Cost-effective groupage solution for smaller shipments

Service Description

LTL combines cargo from multiple clients into a single truck, reducing costs for each shipper. Ideal for shipments between 100 and 10,000 kg that do not require full truck capacity.

Advantages:

  • Lower cost per kilogram
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Weekly departures
  • Full cargo insurance

Service Details

Load capacity:100–10,000 kg
Volume:up to 33 m³
Frequency:Weekly departures
Routes:All EU countries
Delivery time:3–10 business days
Tracking:Online portal
