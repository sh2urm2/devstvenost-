Who We Are

VTK Trible Trailer Inc. is a European freight carrier founded in 2009. We specialize in full truckload (FTL), groupage (LTL), refrigerated, flatbed, and hazardous goods transport across 48 countries.

Our fleet of 47+ vehicles is operated by experienced, certified drivers who understand the complexity of cross-border logistics. From customs clearance to last-mile delivery, we manage the entire process.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, transparent, and on-time freight solutions that help businesses grow without worrying about logistics. We believe trust is built delivery by delivery.
